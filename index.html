Jian Liu (刘 健)

Hello, this is Jian Liu. I’m currently a Ph.D student at NLPR, INSTITUTE OF AUTOMATION, CHNESE ACADEMY OF SCIENCES. Prior to that, I got my master's and bachelor's degree from Northeastern University (NEU).

My research interests include natural language understanding, information extraction, probabilistic modeling, and Bayesian learning.
