Jian Liu (刘 健)

I am currently a Lecturer at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU).

I got my Ph.D degree at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Jun Zhao.

My main research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Information Extraction (IE). I also maintain an active interest in Machine Learning (ML), especially probabilistic Bayesian learning.
