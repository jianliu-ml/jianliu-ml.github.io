Jian Liu (刘 健)

I am currently a Lecturer at Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU). Prior to that, I got my Ph.D degree at Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Jun Zhao, and my master's and bachelor's degree from Northeastern University (NEU), supervised by Prof. Jingbo Zhu.

My main research interests include Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Information Extraction (IE). I also maintain an active interest in Machine Learning (ML), especially probabilistic Bayesian learning.
