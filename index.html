Jian Liu (刘 健)

I am a Ph.D student (since 2016) at INSTITUTE OF AUTOMATION, CHNESE ACADEMY OF SCIENCES, supervised by Jun Zhao. Prior to that, I got my master's and bachelor's degree from Northeastern University (NEU), supervised by Jingbo Zhu.

My research interests include Information Extraction (IE), Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Bayesian Learning (currently).
